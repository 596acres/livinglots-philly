Friendly Owners are created when someone fills in the form you see after clicking "I know of vacant land not on this map" on the main map.

This means that we do not have a lot for a piece of land that someone else (presumably who lives in the neighborhood and knows better than we do) knows is vacant. See the User Guide for more.
